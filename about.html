About the Owner

Our sheep shed is managed by Mr. Sathish Kumar, an experienced livestock farmer with over a decade of hands-on experience in breeding and raising Indian sheep.

With a herd of over 150 sheep, this shed focuses on efficient care, feeding, health monitoring, and record keeping. The project aims to streamline the management of the shed using modern technologies and data systems.
